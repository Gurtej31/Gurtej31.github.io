Workouts

Here are some of our most popular workouts:

  • Circuit Training

    Circuit training is a form of body conditioning or resistance training that targets strength building and muscular endurance. An exercise "circuit" is one completion of all prescribed exercises in the program. At Fitness Freaks, we offer personalized circuit training plans based on your fitness goals.

  • Yoga

    Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India. It is an excellent way to improve flexibility, strength, and balance. We offer a variety of yoga classes for all levels, including hatha, vinyasa, and power yoga.

  • Cardio Kickboxing

    Cardio kickboxing is a high-energy workout that combines martial arts techniques with fast-paced cardio. It's a great way to improve coordination, burn calories, and relieve stress. Our experienced trainers will guide you through every move.
